About the Cyber-Nutrient Terminal

Hi, I'm Misael. I built the Cyber-Nutrient Terminal because I wanted a precise and good-looking calculator.

We're moving towards the future, so why not make the future look like the future? Sci-fi interfaces are all about that.

I designed this terminal to solve the problem that many of us gym-goers encounter when wanting to achieve a physical goal: the problem of knowing your macros. While this problem is already solved by multiple calculators online, mine differs with its unique style.

Whether you are tracking ground beef and oats or planning a complex bulking protocol, this tool is built to provide accurate, actionable data to fuel your goals.
